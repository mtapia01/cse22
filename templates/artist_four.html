Frank Ocean



Why I Like Them

Frank Ocean is an R&B singer that mixes in rap. The beats that he sings/raps over are what caught my attention. Frank Ocean is no different than Juice Wrld or Lil Uzi he also makes sad music. Frank's music is good for when I am driving or I just want to relax.



Songs I Like

I have also been listening to Frank for a long time. I discovered him my freshman year of high school. Some songs I like are Raf, Dear April, Chanel, Nights, Ivy, and White Ferrari. Many people say his album Chanel Orange is one his best works but I never feel in love with it like others have. That album is one of my least favorites albums that he has made.
