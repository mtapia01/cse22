Juice Wrld


Why I Like Them

Before listening to rap I only listened to rock and heavy metal. Juice Wrld was able to still sound like rock even though he was rapping and I liked the way he was able to add a rock style to his songs. Another reason I liked his music is because of the way he effortlessly freestyled. He was able to make a song in less than five minutes which I thought was shocking.



Songs I Like

Juice Wrld's music is usually sad songs talking about dying or breaking up with girls. But occasionally you can find a happy song talking about him spending all his money or how he lives care free. Some songs I like are Used To, Wasted, Up Up & Away, and XO.
