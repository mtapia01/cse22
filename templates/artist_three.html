A Day To Remember

Why I Like Them

Unlike the other people on my list this is a rock band. I have been listening to this band since the fifth grade. I have not listened to their new releases I only go back to the older albums. A Day to Remeber has loud sounds that can get you pumped up. But they do have the occasionally slow sad song. I like how they are able to sing and be heard without all the screaming some rock bands do.



Songs I Like

My list of song have changed throughout the years. I now only manage to go back to a couple songs which are Have Faith in Me, All I Want, Since U Been Gone, Right Where You Want Me to Be, and If It Means a Lot to You. I like these song because they are easy to relax to and just enjoy because they have little to no screaming.
