Lil Uzi Vert

Why I Like Them

Just like Juice Wrld Lil Uzi is able to add rock influence to his songs. His style is the same as Juice in that they both make sad songs but with Lil Uzi you will easily find happy songs. Another thing I like about his music is the people he is able to collaborate with.



Songs I Like

Lil Uzi is one of the first rapper I ever listened to. Some songs I like are Of Course we Ghetto Flowers, Do What I Want, The Way Life Goes, and Buy It. These songs are both sad and happy. He was able to do both well and that is what keeps me listening to him.
